# Privacy Policy for Local Legend FC

**Effective date:** June 24, 2026

[Home]({{ '/' | relative_url }}) · [Support]({{ '/support/' | relative_url }})

## Overview

Local Legend FC is fully offline. No personal data is collected or sent. The app does not require an account, include advertising, use analytics, track users, or provide network features.

## Data We Collect

Local Legend FC does not collect, store on external servers, sell, share, or send personal data. The app does not use tracking technologies or third-party analytics.

## Local Saves

Game saves and settings are stored locally on the user's device. They are used only to run the game and are not sent to the developer. Deleting the app may also delete its local saves. Local Legend FC does not provide cloud backup or account-based save recovery.

## Children's Privacy

Local Legend FC is not designed to collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because the app has no accounts, analytics, advertising, tracking, or network features, it does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If a parent or guardian has a question about this policy, they may use the contact information below.

## Third-Party Services

The app itself does not integrate third-party advertising, analytics, tracking, account, or social-network services.

Apple may process download, purchase, TestFlight, device, or crash-related information as part of operating the App Store and TestFlight. That processing is controlled by Apple and is subject to Apple's own privacy policies. Local Legend FC does not receive personal data from the app itself.
