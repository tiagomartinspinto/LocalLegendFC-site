Local Legend FC is a premium offline retro football management game about building a fictional local club into a local legend.

Choose a footballing home, guide your squad through league and cup matches, make tactical changes at half-time, manage the club's limited resources, and build a legacy season by season.

**Paid app. No ads. No in-app purchases at launch.**

## Private by Design

Local Legend FC is fully offline. No personal data is collected or sent. The app has no accounts, analytics, advertising, tracking, or network features. Saves stay on the user's device.
