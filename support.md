# Local Legend FC Support

Local Legend FC is a free retro indie football management game about building a fictional local club into a local legend.

[Home]({{ '/' | relative_url }}) · [Privacy Policy]({{ '/privacy/' | relative_url }})

**Support contact:** LocalLegendFC@protonmail.com

When contacting support, include the app version, iPhone model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened. Do not send sensitive personal information.

## The App Does Not Open

1. Close Local Legend FC completely and open it again.
2. Restart the iPhone.
3. Check that iOS and Local Legend FC are up to date.
4. Make sure the device has free storage available.
5. If the problem continues, contact support with the details listed above.

Reinstalling the app should be a last resort because it may permanently remove local saves.

## Saves

Local Legend FC stores up to five save slots locally on the device. Saves are not attached to an account and are not synced through the game to another device. The app does not provide cloud restoration or remote recovery.

Matches are saved after full time rather than during an active match. If the app closes mid-match, progress from that match may need to be replayed.

## Sound and Haptics

Sound Effects and Haptics can be turned on or off in Settings. If sound is enabled but cannot be heard:

1. Check the in-game Sound Effects setting.
2. Check the iPhone volume and Silent mode.
3. Confirm audio is not being routed to headphones or another device.

Haptics may vary by device and system settings. Check both the in-game Haptics setting and the iPhone's haptic settings.

## Deleting Saves

Open **Save Slots**, find the save you want to remove, and use its delete option. If a game is currently loaded, Settings may also show a clearly identified reset option for that active save.

Deleting a save is permanent and cannot be undone. Deleting the app may remove all local saves.

## Privacy

Local Legend FC is fully offline. No personal data is collected or sent. It has no accounts, analytics, advertising, tracking, or network features. Saves remain on the user's device.

## Fictional-World Disclaimer

Local Legend FC is a work of fiction. Clubs, players, towns, stadiums, competitions, football organizations, and match events are fictional. Real country and region names are used only as geographic setting. The app is not affiliated with or endorsed by any real club, league, player, governing body, or other organization.
